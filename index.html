Cultural Fit, A Manifesto

  • We believe that a great cultural fit makes employees lives better.
  • The best products and services are created where the company and employ are strongly aligned.
  • We value the complexity of human diversity and businesses that seek to improve the human experience.
  • Our work defines us more than we often realize.
  • Reflection and mindfulness are necessary for people to grow and improve. Successful companies and people embrace self-awareness and change.
  • Toxicity is the result of a poor fit between a company’s culture and its employees’ values. Positive employee engagement and experience fosters the creation of great products and services.
  • Qualitative understanding and quantitative assessments of culture and behaviors lead to better products, employee experiences and profits.
